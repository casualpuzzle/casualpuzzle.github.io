Privacy Policy - Cartoon World

Welcome to Cartoon World! We take your privacy very seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand the policies and practices we follow when we collect, use, and share your personal information.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

  • Device Information: We collect information about your device type, operating system version, browser type, and language.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you use our game, such as the games you play, the ads you click on, and the features you use.
  • Advertising Information: We collect information about the ads you see and how you interact with them.
  • Firebase Information: We use Firebase to analyze your game usage. Firebase collects your device ID, IP address, and other anonymous information.

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

Improve Game Experience: We use your information to improve the game experience, such as by providing you with personalized ads and game content.

Analytics and Statistics: We use your information for analytics and statistics to understand how users use our game and improve our services.

Ad Serving: We use your information to show you relevant ads.

3. Your Choices

You can choose not to share your information with us. You can achieve this by:

  • Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising: You can choose to opt-out of personalized advertising, which means we will not use your information to show you relevant ads.
  • Uninstall the Game: You can uninstall the game to delete all the information we have collected.
