We take your privacy very seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand the policies and practices we follow when we collect, use, and share your personal information.

What information do we collect?

  • Device Information: We collect information about your device type, operating system version, browser type, and language.

  • Advertising Information: We collect information about the ads you see and how you interact with them.
